# üé≠ Karma Nexus - Trait Characters & Abilities Guide

## Overview

In Karma Nexus, traits are not just passive statistics - they are **active abilities** that define your character's capabilities and playstyle. Each trait can be equipped and used to perform unique actions that affect yourself, other players, and the game world.

---

## üìã Trait System Mechanics

### Trait Types
1. **Skills (20)** - Active abilities that can be learned and improved
2. **Superpower Tools (25)** - Rare powerful abilities with significant effects
3. **Meta Traits (20)** - Advanced traits that modify gameplay mechanics
4. **Virtues (20)** - Good traits that provide beneficial abilities
5. **Vices (20)** - Dark traits that provide powerful but risky abilities

### Acquisition Methods
- **Discovery**: Find trait items randomly spawned in the game world
- **Investment**: Pay coins to acquire or upgrade the trait
- **Wait Time**: Must wait at the location to complete acquisition
- **Rarity**: Higher rarity = longer wait time + higher cost

### Equipping & Usage
- **Equipment Slots**: Players can equip up to 6 active traits simultaneously
- **Activation**: Click/activate equipped traits to use their abilities
- **Cooldown**: Each ability has a cooldown period (30 seconds to 24 hours)
- **Energy Cost**: Using abilities consumes energy/stamina
- **Level Requirements**: Some abilities require minimum trait levels

## üéÆ Gameplay Mechanics Summary

### Trait Acquisition Process
1. **Discovery**: Random spawns in world (every 15-60 minutes depending on rarity)
2. **Approach**: Get within proximity (50m) to see trait item
3. **Interaction**: Click trait icon to see details (name, cost, rarity, stats)
4. **Purchase Decision**: Decide if you want to invest
5. **Investment**: Pay credits and begin wait timer
6. **Waiting**: Must stay within area for duration (1-10 minutes)
7. **Completion**: Trait acquired or upgraded

### Trait Management
- **Equipped Slots**: 6 active trait slots
- **Trait Library**: Store all owned traits
- **Swapping**: Can change equipped traits (5 minute cooldown)
- **Upgrading**: Find same trait again to level it up
- **Max Level**: All traits max at level 100

### Combat Integration
- **Hotkeys**: Assign traits to hotkeys 1-6
- **Cooldowns**: Visual indicators show ability availability
- **Energy**: Energy bar shows current/max energy
- **Combos**: Certain trait combinations create powerful synergies

### Social Dynamics
- **Karma System**: Every action affects karma (-100 to +100)
- **Reputation**: Separate from karma, based on community perception
- **Factions**: Different traits favor different factions
- **Player Relationships**: Traits affect how others see and interact with you

### Economic Impact
- **Trait Market**: Players can trade trait items
- **Scarcity**: Legendary traits very rare, high demand
- **Investment**: Credits spent on traits are permanent investments
- **ROI**: Powerful traits enable better earning potential
